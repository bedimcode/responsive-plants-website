How our process
is environmentally friendly

The following pictures visually show the problems that Souper Fresh fix. Buy and enjoy best quality while also helping the environment.

Whey is a by-product of cheese making

Farmhouse cheesemakers pay to get rid of it

or spread it on the land if they have some

While soup makers have to pay for stock
or make their own form simmering bones

Resulting in a soup with very high salt content

and a production process that requires large amounts of energy and water, contributing to carbon emissions and water pollution.

Souper Fresh mitigates all the negative effects of traditional soup-making, while also saving cheesemakers money and promoting the quality of their land. We are dedicated to supplying the highest quality soup on the market, by providing customers with a product that is far more nutricious than our competitive counterparts, and with a much more environmentally friendly production process.
